## Tier-by-tier feature unlocks

**Free** — website visitor ID (people+company), premium enrichment 20+ providers, 2 automated sequences (email/LinkedIn/power dialer).

**Launch ($159)** — Free + unlimited AI research agents, real-time buying intent signals, unlimited sequences, CRM + Slack integrations, API access for B2B contact data.

**Scale ($399)** — Launch + shared team workspace, multi-line parallel phone dialer (unlimited numbers), advanced SalesGPT deep-research queries.

**Copilot ($799)** — Scale + dedicated onboarding, priority Slack support, fully managed email+LinkedIn infra setup, priority access to new features.

**Enterprise** — custom credit packages, volume discounts, dedicated GTM engineer, SLA + security review, custom workflow, "fully autonomous sales execution".

## Comparison copy

- "Traditional Legacy Tools" (ZoomInfo, 6Sense, Nooks, Instantly, Heyreach): $400–$1500+/yr/seat, annual contracts, "workflows are broken", "minimal AI".
- "GTM Agencies": $5,000+/mo to set up + manage; "you don't own your revenue infrastructure".
- FuseAI: from $159/mo, 50k credits "inbuilt for tasks" (note: inconsistent — pricing table shows 60k at Launch), setup in minutes.

## Observations PM

- Credit model = unified currency across prospecting + engagement + signals + AI research. Unusual and clever: most competitors price seats × features. Fuse prices **tasks**, regardless of channel.
- High-cost-per-action items reveal economics: phone enrichment (200 cr) = ~10× email. Signal agents (100 cr) priced as premium feature.
- "API access" gated to Launch ($159) — Free tier is product-only.
- Unlimited seats from Scale upward is aggressive vs. per-seat incumbents (Outreach $100-160/seat).
- "Contact Save/Search" at 2 credits/each → even browsing the DB burns credits. Soft lock-in via consumption.
- CTA for Free/Launch/Scale all = "Get started for free" → self-serve signup; Copilot/Enterprise = "Contact Sales". Wedge = self-serve up to $399.
- No mention of email warmup, deliverability infra in tiers < Copilot. "Fully managed email/LinkedIn infra" is $799 feature.
- Annual 25% off visible as toggle. Launch $159 → ~$119/mo annual. Confirms public pricing in user brief.
- No free trial on paid tiers; Free → paid is pure upgrade.
