# Healthtech playbook

Tenant ICP : digital health, healthcare SaaS, clinical workflow tools, telemedicine platforms, healthtech infrastructure (claims, EHR integrations).

## TAM seed queries

- `industry: "Hospital & Health Care" OR "Healthtech" OR "Medical Device"`
- `keywords: "EHR" OR "clinical workflow" OR "telehealth" OR "patient engagement"`
- `funded between $10M-$100M`
- `HIPAA-compliant SaaS in product copy or trust center`

## Buying signals (5 canonical)

| Signal | Detection | Why it fires |
|---|---|---|
| **hipaa_compliance_milestone** | Press / trust-center announcement of SOC2 + HIPAA | Maturity → enterprise outbound budget |
| **provider_partnership** | New hospital system / IDN partnership | Distribution event — needs outbound for cross-sell |
| **clinical_lead_hired** | Chief Medical Officer / VP Clinical Strategy joins | Clinical credibility hire → GTM scaling |
| **rfp_response_active** | LinkedIn job post mentioning "RFP coordinator" | High-intent enterprise sale pipeline forming |
| **fda_or_regulatory_clearance** | FDA 510(k) clearance, CE mark | Regulatory unlock = market entry |

## Sequence templates (3)

### Sequence A — "Compliance-first founder note"
```
Step 1 (D+0):
Subject: "On <HIPAA milestone> — sales scaling next?"
Body: Recognise compliance achievement. Most healthtech founders unlock outbound only after this. Offer 15-min on outbound architecture for HIPAA-aware orgs.

Step 2 (D+5):
Subject: "Audit trail walkthrough"
Body: 3-min Loom showing audit log + DPA template.

Step 3 (D+12):
Subject: "If now is not the right moment"
Body: Soft break-up. Quarterly check-in invitation.
```

### Sequence B — "RFP / health-system selling"
```
Step 1 (D+0):
Subject: "RFP cycle management — how <comparable> handles it"
Body: Recognise RFP coordinator role. Outbound that surfaces multi-stakeholder buying committees in healthtech is rare.

Step 2 (D+7):
Subject: "Stakeholder map automation"
Body: Show how Elevay automates the 6-12 stakeholder mapping that RFPs require.

Step 3 (D+14):
Subject: "Last note"
Body: Long-cycle nurture (healthtech enterprise sale = 9-18 months).
```

### Sequence C — "Provider partnership amplifier"
```
Step 1 (D+0):
Subject: "Re: <hospital partnership> — IDN expansion"
Body: Recognise partnership. Offer to share how comparable healthtech expanded across IDN sister hospitals.

Step 2 (D+6):
Subject: "IDN cross-sell playbook"
Body: 1-pager.

Step 3 (D+15):
Subject: "Stepping back"
Body: Long nurture.
```

## Pipeline stages

1. **Discovery**
2. **Clinical champion identified**
3. **HIPAA + security review** — BAA, SOC2, encryption-at-rest
4. **Procurement** — health-system procurement is byzantine, 3-6 months
5. **Legal + BAA executed**
6. **Pilot** — single department / single clinic
7. **Won / Lost**

## Common objections

- *"We can't have any patient data leak risk."* → Elevay never touches PHI ; outbound stays in CRM-side data.
- *"Procurement takes 6 months."* → Scope a self-serve pilot for the SaaS layer that bypasses health-system procurement (founder-level decision).
- *"We need a BAA."* → Provide template within 24h.

## Geo notes

- US : largest healthtech market. State Medicaid variation matters. NY, MA, CA sweet spots.
- UK / NHS : 12-18 month cycle but high LTV. Skip in early-stage outreach.
- EU (DE, NL, NO) : socialised systems are public-procurement heavy. Skip unless founder has a pre-existing path.
