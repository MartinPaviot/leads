# Fintech playbook

Tenant ICP : fintech infrastructure (payments, banking-as-a-service, treasury, lending, compliance), challenger banks, embedded finance.

## TAM seed queries

- `industry: "Financial Services" OR "Fintech"`
- `keywords: "payments platform" OR "banking infrastructure" OR "embedded finance" OR "lending"`
- `funded between $10M-$200M` (fintech needs more capital than devtools)
- `headcount 25-500`

## Buying signals (5 canonical)

| Signal | Detection | Why it fires |
|---|---|---|
| **regulatory_milestone** | License / charter granted (state, FCA, BaFin) — press releases | Regulatory clearance unlocks growth playbooks |
| **funding_with_growth_explicit_focus** | Round announcement mentions "international expansion" / "scale GTM" | Direct GTM tooling budget signal |
| **chro_or_revenue_lead_hired** | New CRO / VP Sales / Chief Revenue Officer | First-90-days outbound investment |
| **compliance_hire_burst** | ≥3 compliance / risk hires | Pre-IPO maturity, sales motion forming |
| **partnership_announcement** | Press release with a new platform partner | Distribution moment — outbound efficiency matters |

## Sequence templates (3)

### Sequence A — "Regulatory milestone congrats"
```
Step 1 (D+0):
Subject: "On the <license/charter> — outbound to <expanded geo>?"
Body: Recognise the milestone. Reference one fintech-equivalent that scaled outbound after a similar event. Offer 15-min.

Step 2 (D+4):
Subject: "<Geo> outbound playbook"
Body: 1-pager attached. Specific to fintech regulatory complexity in that geo.

Step 3 (D+10):
Subject: "Stepping back"
Body: Soft break-up + customer ROI bullet.
```

### Sequence B — "Compliance complexity selling"
```
Step 1 (D+0):
Subject: "How <comparable fintech> ran outbound under SOC2"
Body: Compliance-friendly outbound architecture. Most fintech founders worry about deliverability + audit trail.

Step 2 (D+5):
Subject: "Audit trail demo (3 min)"
Body: Loom showing how Elevay's audit log meets fintech requirements.

Step 3 (D+12):
Subject: "Last note"
Body: Soft break-up.
```

### Sequence C — "Cross-border expansion"
```
Step 1 (D+0):
Subject: "<Geo expansion> + GTM stack"
Body: Recognise their expansion plan (from press / hiring). Offer to share what comparable fintech's stack looked like.

Step 2 (D+6):
Subject: "Re: <geo> stack"
Body: Bump with one comparison point.

Step 3 (D+14):
Subject: "If timing was off"
Body: Long-cycle nurture — fintech expansion is 6-12 months.
```

## Pipeline stages

1. **Discovery**
2. **Champion + use case** — concrete outbound problem
3. **Compliance review** — vendor security + DPA
4. **Pilot scoped** — pre-paid trial, 1-2 markets
5. **Procurement / Legal** — AWS PrivateLink / DPA / SOC2 review
6. **Won / Lost**

## Common objections

- *"We can't use US-hosted SaaS for EU customers."* → DPA + EU residency option (when it ships) + SCC.
- *"Compliance won't approve outbound automation."* → Audit log + opt-out compliance + GDPR-aware footer.
- *"We have a CRM."* → Position as outbound-execution layer above CRM, not replacement.

## Geo notes

- UK : largest fintech hub in EU. Cycle 6-10 weeks. FCA-aware messaging mandatory.
- US : NY, SF, Miami. Cycles 4-8 weeks. State licensing complexity in the message.
- DE / NL : BaFin / DNB awareness. Cycles 8-14 weeks. German-language nurture content.
- FR : ACPR-aware. Smaller market but high deal sizes.
- Asia (Singapore) : MAS sandbox graduates. High velocity once entered.
