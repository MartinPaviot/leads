# E-commerce / DTC playbook

Tenant ICP : Shopify-app makers, Amazon-seller tooling, DTC brand SaaS (loyalty, returns, A/B testing, post-purchase), retail-tech infrastructure.

## TAM seed queries

- `keywords: "Shopify app" OR "DTC brand SaaS" OR "post-purchase"`
- `app store presence : Shopify / BigCommerce / WooCommerce`
- `funded between $2M-$30M` (smaller checks, faster cycles)
- `headcount 5-100`

## Buying signals (5 canonical)

| Signal | Detection | Why it fires |
|---|---|---|
| **app_store_install_velocity** | Install count growth ≥30% MoM | Demand pull — outbound budget unlocks |
| **funding_seed_to_a** | Seed → A round in last 90d | First GTM hire being recruited |
| **shopify_plus_partner_status** | Achieved Shopify Plus partner badge | Enterprise tier — cross-sell to bigger brands |
| **head_of_growth_hired** | New Head of Growth / VP Marketing | First-90-days outbound |
| **public_metrics_milestone** | "10k merchants installed" press / tweet | Maturity moment, often paired with outbound investment |

## Sequence templates (3)

### Sequence A — "Install velocity congrats"
```
Step 1 (D+0):
Subject: "30% MoM growth — outbound to enterprise next?"
Body: Recognise install velocity. Most Shopify-app founders only start outbound to enterprise brands at this point. Offer 15-min.

Step 2 (D+3):
Subject: "Enterprise brand outbound for app makers"
Body: 1-pager — how the top 10 Shopify apps moved upmarket.

Step 3 (D+7):
Subject: "Stepping back"
Body: Soft break-up + invitation to monthly DTC SaaS founder Slack (community).
```

### Sequence B — "Shopify Plus expansion"
```
Step 1 (D+0):
Subject: "On Plus partner status — D2C brand TAM"
Body: Recognise milestone. Concrete brand list (50 Plus brands matching their use case).

Step 2 (D+4):
Subject: "Enterprise brand intro paths"
Body: Show how warm-intro inference works on Shopify Plus brands.

Step 3 (D+10):
Subject: "Last note"
Body: Soft.
```

### Sequence C — "Funding-event nurture"
```
Step 1 (D+0):
Subject: "Congrats on the round — first hire?"
Body: Founder-to-founder. Reference round. Single concrete benchmark.

Step 2 (D+5):
Subject: "Re: first GTM hire"
Body: One angle on outbound automation as a leverage point pre-Head-of-Growth.

Step 3 (D+12):
Subject: "Stepping back"
Body: Soft break-up.
```

## Pipeline stages

1. **Discovery**
2. **Champion identified** (founder or growth lead)
3. **Trial** — most DTC SaaS = 14-30 day trial
4. **Procurement** — usually founder card, fast
5. **Won / Lost**

## Common objections

- *"We're too small for a CRM."* → Position as outbound execution, not data warehouse. Self-serve onboarding wizard handles their 50-account TAM.
- *"We have Klaviyo / Postscript / Yotpo."* → Email/SMS to existing customers is downstream of Elevay's outbound to NEW merchants.
- *"Founder does sales themselves."* → Wizard lets the founder DO sales themselves with the AI assist — not a CRM replacement.

## Geo notes

- US : largest DTC market. NY, LA, Austin sweet spots.
- UK / EU : DTC mature in UK + DE + NL + DK. Mid-cycle (4-6 weeks).
- LATAM : emerging. Smaller deal sizes but very fast cycles.
- Skip APAC unless founder has a path.
