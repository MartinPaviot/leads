# RAW: the full CRO job → AI CRO-Copilot surface area (first-principles)
*Stream 4, 2026-06-14. The real job is ~11 domains; Monaco's marketed copilot is ~15% of it.*

**CRO answers four questions on a loop:** Will we hit the number? (forecast) → Do we have enough, is it real? (pipeline) → Which deals, how do we win? (deals) → Right people/plays/territories/comp? (system) — wrapped in board accountability above + retention/expansion as the 2nd engine.

## 1. Forecasting & board reporting
forecast call (challenge each number; expose sandbag vs happy-ears) · category assignment w/ evidence (Commit but no EB → flag) · roll-up rep→mgr→segment→co w/ override tracking · accuracy/calibration per rep (bias model, auto-adjust) · quota attainment + leading-indicator miss prediction · slippage/pull-forward (snapshot diff) · board deck revenue section (numbers+narrative) · investor narrative (bull/base/bear, vs prior guidance) · scenario/what-if (close X + hire Y → land where).
*AI needs:* time-series opp snapshots, per-rep forecast history, quotas, ramp curves, capacity model.

## 2. Pipeline management
pipeline review (per-rep pre-read) · coverage (**true = quota÷win-rate**, not 3x) + forward gap alert · PG accountability (new-opps vs target by source/owner) · stage hygiene (past-due, stuck, zombie, missing fields) · inspection cadence (rhythm + follow-up) · velocity/aging vs baseline · inflow/outflow waterfall.

## 3. Deal management
deal reviews (per-deal brief from all touchpoints) · **MEDDPICC inspection** (auto-extract + score + gaps; paper process kills ~28%) · deal strategy (gaps+competitor+stakeholders→moves) · risk calls (engagement-signal model on closed history) · save plays (intervention recommender + exec draft) · exec sponsorship (which deals + prep brief) · **MAP** (auto-draft, slippage track; buying groups 6–10) · **multi-thread/stakeholder map** (single-thread flag, missing roles) · competitive positioning (mention→battlecard).

## 4. Rep performance & coaching
1:1 pre-reads (one theme + evidence) · scorecards (auto-scored rubric, trends) · ramp vs cohort (flag off-track; ~50% faster w/ coaching loops) · PIP dossiers + early "trending to PIP" · skill-gap → micro-coaching · call coaching (coachable moments; ~80% of coaching time is observation) · **AI role-play partner** · talk-track design (mine top-performer calls; A/B messaging vs wins).

## 5. GTM strategy
ICP (derive from won vs lost/churn) · segmentation (cluster on fit+economics) · territory design (balance potential/count/geo/capacity) · quota (bottoms-up from potential+attainment+ramp) · capacity (target→pipe→reps→hire-by dates) · comp design (simulate; detect perverse incentives; tie to NRR) · channel/partner · pricing/packaging input (discount/elasticity from win-loss).

## 6. Process & methodology
methodology selection (fit to complexity/ACV/cycle) · **enforcement** (auto-fill fields so adoption isn't manual — the #1 unsolved GTM-leader problem) · playbooks (contextual surfacing) · sequences/cadences (generate+optimize, behavior-adaptive) · enablement content (auto battlecards/objection from call data) · onboarding (personalized path + knowledge assistant).

## 7. Revenue operations
data hygiene (auto-capture/dedupe/complete/anomaly) · tooling/integration health · attribution (first/last/MTA/MMM) · funnel conversion (leak + impact sizing) · **win/loss program** (themes from conversation+outcome → coaching/forecast/product) · deal desk/approvals (guardrails, routing, margin flags) · reporting (NL ask-anything, cited, governed warehouse).

## 8. Demand & marketing alignment
sourcing mix (mkt/sales/partner/expansion balance + gap predict) · marketing SLA (MQL→SQL, response time, quality by source) · ABM (fit×intent + orchestrated plays) · **signal-based prospecting** ("why now": job change/funding/hiring/tech/intent/usage → ranked + auto-trigger) · demand-gen feedback (campaign→revenue ROI).

## 9. Customer expansion & retention (2nd revenue engine)
NRR ownership (bridge: expansion−churn−contraction; world-class 110–120%+) · churn-risk (usage decline/support sentiment/**sponsor departure**/single-thread/engagement drop) · expansion plays (seat saturation/feature adoption/new use cases/hiring → triggered) · QBR auto-deck (value/usage/ROI/expansion/risk) · renewal forecast (+ GRR separate so expansion doesn't mask churn) · sales↔CS handoff (shared context, collision/ownership, NRR joint KPI).

## 10. People & leadership  *(assist-only — most human-resistant)*
hiring (scorecard eval, success-profile from top-rep traits, headcount→capacity link) · interviewing (structured kits) · org design (model structures/ratios/span) · culture (engagement/fairness signals) · cross-functional leadership (single source of revenue truth + per-stakeholder briefs).
*AI = augmentation (dossiers, rubrics, fairness checks), not replacement — aligns "machine reveals, human acts" / "AE stays human".*

## Master taxonomy (ranked by revenue impact; automation ceiling; data dep)
| Rank | Domain | Why | Ceiling | Data |
|---|---|---|---|---|
|1|**Forecasting & predictability**|the number gates every decision|auto-forecast+calibrate+roll-up; **human owns commit**|time-series snapshots, forecast history, quotas|
|2|**Deal execution & inspection**|where revenue is won/lost|auto-inspect/risk/MAP/play; **human runs deal**|conversation capture, stakeholder map|
|3|**Pipeline management**|no pipe→no forecast|mostly automatable (hygiene/coverage/velocity/pre-reads)|opp lifecycle, activity, stage criteria|
|4|**Expansion & retention/NRR**|at scale NRR>new-logo|risk/expansion/QBR/renewal automatable; human runs relationship|usage, support, contract, exec engagement|
|5|**Rep performance & coaching**|compounds every rep|scoring/scorecards/role-play/1:1 automatable; human delivers hard feedback|recorded calls, outcomes, longitudinal perf|
|6|**Demand & PG**|fills the top|highly automatable (signals/prioritize/outreach/SLA)|enrichment, intent, behavioral, lead lifecycle|
|7|**RevOps & analytics**|the trusted substrate|hygiene/capture/NL-analytics/win-loss automatable; deal-desk needs guardrails|governed revenue warehouse|
|8|**GTM strategy**|sets the ceiling|AI proposes/models; **human decides**|TAM scoring, attainment, capacity|
|9|**Process & methodology**|makes it repeatable|enforcement via auto-fill + playbook surfacing|conversation→fields, play library|
|10|**Board/investor reporting**|high-visibility, downstream|deck+narrative automatable; human owns the room|all above + prior decks|
|11|**People & leadership**|long-run capacity|**assist-only**|perf correlates, hiring plan, engagement|

**Two structural enablers under everything:** (a) **universal interaction capture (zero entry)** — every domain degrades to guesswork without it; (b) **NL query + proactive intelligence over a governed warehouse** — what Monaco markets as the *whole* copilot is actually just the **interface** on top of 1–11.

**Re-audit method:** score each domain 0–3 (0 absent · 1 shows · 2 recommends · 3 closes loop). Monaco's marketed copilot ≈ 1–2 on domains 1–3, near-silent on 4–11. The gap between "3 bullets" and "11 domains × loop-closure" is the true scope.
